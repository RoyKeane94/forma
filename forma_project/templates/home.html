Questions

Want to know more?

Will my clients actually film themselves?

The ones who love training with you will. The link opens straight to camera with three prompts on screen, so there's nothing to figure out and nothing to download. The best moment to ask is right after a milestone: a goal hit, a race finished, a six month anniversary. Forma prompts you at exactly those moments.

What if I don't like a video?

Nothing goes live without your approval. Every submission lands in your dashboard first, where you watch it, pick a pull quote, and decide. You're in control of everything on your page.

How is this different from Google reviews?

A Google review is a star rating from a profile nobody can verify. A Forma testimonial is a real client, on camera, with their name and job, talking about a specific outcome. It can't be faked at scale, and it answers the question a star rating never does: someone like me, with my problem, got a result.

Do I need a website?

No. Your Proof page is a complete, professional page on its own: your photo, where you train, your specialisms, and your testimonials. If you do have a website, you can embed your Proof wall on it with one line of code.

I'm fully booked. Why would I need this?

That's exactly when to capture it. Right now you have no permanent record of what you've built. When a client moves away, your rates go up, or a corporate asks for references, you'd be starting from zero. Forma records your reputation while you're at your best, not when you need it.

How much does it cost?

£29 a month, with the first month free and no commitment. There's no commission and no cut of your sessions. One client converted by your Proof page covers the subscription for the year.
